## 🎯 Executive Summary

**CENTURION (100Cr Engine)** is India's first revenue milestone prediction platform designed specifically for Indian SaaS founders. It answers the critical question: *"When will my business reach ₹100 Crore in annual revenue?"*

### Core Value Proposition
- **Instant Projections** — Calculate your path to ₹100Cr in seconds
- **Benchmark Intelligence** — Compare your growth to 500+ Indian SaaS founders
- **Monthly Tracking** — Log actual revenue and track progress vs projections
- **AI Coaching** — Personalized growth advice powered by Claude AI
